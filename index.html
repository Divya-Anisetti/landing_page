Our Coffee Types

Lactose-Free

Smooth and creamy coffee without the lactose. Perfect for those with lactose intolerance or who simply prefer a lighter taste.

  • Ingredients: Arabica beans, Lactose-free milk
  • Flavor Profile: Mild, Creamy
  • Best Served: Hot or iced

Black

Rich, bold coffee for purists who love the pure, unadulterated taste of coffee. A robust choice for any time of day.

  • Ingredients: 100% Arabica beans
  • Flavor Profile: Strong, Bitter
  • Best Served: Black, with a splash of water or milk

Latte

Creamy and delicious, our Latte combines espresso with steamed milk for a smooth and comforting beverage.

  • Ingredients: Espresso, Steamed milk, Froth
  • Flavor Profile: Smooth, Creamy
  • Best Served: Hot with a sprinkle of cocoa powder
